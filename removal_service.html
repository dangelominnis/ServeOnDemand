WE PAY CASH FOR PALLETS!

Whether your pallets are broken beyond repair or just need a little TLC,
Serve On Demand® can provide a new home or use for them.

For pallets that can be repurposed, we will purchase them at a fair market price. Depending on your location and volumes, we’ll even pick up scrap pallets at little or no cost.

Because Serve On Demand® is committed to the preservation of both our natural resources and business collateral, we actively seek cost-effective ways to maintain the supply chain. This also allows us to meet our customers needs without sacrificing the high-quality product they have come to expect.

No longer do you have to wonder who will buy your used pallets, dunnage, crates, barrels, drums or odd size pallets. Serve On Demand® will develop a program specific to your needs, all while working together with your business to ensure environmental sustainability.
